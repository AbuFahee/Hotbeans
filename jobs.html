Web Developer

Becoming a web developer requires a diverse set of technical and non-technical skills to design, develop, and maintain websites and web applications. The skills needed encompass a wide range of disciplines, from coding and programming to design, problem-solving, and communication

Qualification/Skills: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent industry experience.

Web Designer

A web designer is responsible for creating the visual and interactive aspects of a website. This role combines creative design skills with technical knowledge to produce visually appealing and user-friendly web interfaces. .

Qualification/Skills: Familiarity with back-end technologies, such as server-side scripting languages (e.g., PHP, Python, Node.js), and databases (e.g., MySQL, MongoDB), can be an advantage for collaborating with developers.

Digital Marketer

A Digital Marketer plays a crucial role in the promotion and growth of businesses and brands in the digital landscape. Their responsibilities span various online channels and strategies to reach, engage, and convert target audiences

Qualification/Skills: Bachelor's degree in marketing, business, communications, or a related field. While a degree is valuable, some digital marketers have successful careers with alternative educational paths or certifications.
